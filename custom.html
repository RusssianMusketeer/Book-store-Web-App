Local events

Our bookstore actively engages the community by hosting local author events, facilitating diverse book clubs, and partnering with schools for literacy programs, making us a vibrant hub for readers and neighbors alike.

Children storytime

Join us every wednesday for Story Time Adventures! We'll share delightful tales, sing songs, and spark young imaginations with interactive fun. Perfect for our littlest readers aged 4-8 and their families! Free to attend, no registration needed.

Events and collaborations

More than just shelves of books, Book Haven Bookstore is a vibrant community hub. Our diverse events—from author readings and engaging workshops to beloved children's story times—offer something for everyone. We also actively build our community through collaborations with local schools, libraries, and businesses, fostering literacy and enriching cultural experiences.
